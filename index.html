About Me

Quantum Explorer & AI Researcher

I'm pursuing an Honors degree in Physics at IIT Bombay with a Minor in Machine Intelligence and Data Science. My research focuses on the fascinating intersection of quantum mechanics and artificial intelligence, particularly using Non-Equilibrium Green's Function (NEGF) formalism to model electron transport in nanoscale systems.

From implementing Physics-Informed Neural Networks that embed the Schrödinger equation directly into machine learning models to analyzing eclipsing binary systems, I bridge theoretical physics with computational intelligence to unlock the secrets of our universe.
